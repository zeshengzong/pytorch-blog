# Join the PyTorch Foundation Ambassador Program: A Global Network of Community Leaders

**By PyTorch Foundation**
**May 22, 2026**

A little over a year ago, the PyTorch Foundation launched the Ambassador Program, an initiative that recognizes and supports independent, trusted voices in the PyTorch community who are passionate about building, educating, and advocating for PyTorch. The aim of the program is to support regional PyTorch communities, recognize technical contributions and enable global knowledge sharing and collaboration.

We are once again on the lookout for more PyTorch enthusiasts to join the program! This time, We especially encourage applications from contributors across Africa, Latin America, the Middle East, Oceania, Southeast Asia, and Eastern Europe as we continue expanding Ambassador representation across more regions and local communities.

## What does it take to become a PyTorch Foundation Ambassador?

PyTorch Foundation Ambassadors play a pivotal role in helping others get started with PyTorch Foundation projects through the work they do – be it mentoring users, creating technical tutorials or organizing local events. Ambassadors are expected to participate in at least one of the following focus areas on a regular basis. The commitment is for a two year term:

* Organize PyTorch Foundation project-focused events, both virtual and in-person
* Create technical tutorials, blog posts, and videos
* Mentor new users and encourage inclusive participation
* Represent PyTorch Foundation at conferences, meetups, and academic institutions

## What do you get as a PyTorch Foundation Ambassador?

The Program provides a range of resources and opportunities to support you as an Ambassador:

* Recognition and Visibility
* Official designation as a PyTorch Foundation Ambassador
* Featured profile on the PyTorch Foundation website
* Promotion through PyTorch social media and communications channels
* Exclusive Access
* Private collaboration channels with fellow Ambassadors and Foundation staff
* Invitations to briefings, workshops, and leadership training
* Event planning toolkits and templates
* Community and Event Support
* Reimbursement for approved community activities and travel
* Complimentary admission to PyTorch Conference
* PyTorch Foundation branded materials and Ambassador kits
* Professional Development
* Opportunities to speak at industry and Foundation events
* Recognition for top contributors
* Networking with machine learning leaders across the globe

## How do you nominate someone?

Nominations are open now through June 18, 2026. Individuals can nominate themselves or someone else. All applications will be reviewed by the PyTorch Foundation team, and selected Ambassadors will be notified in September and officially announced in October 2026.

To apply, visit the PyTorch Ambassador Program Application Page and click on the button that says [Learn More and Apply](https://pytorch.org/programs/ambassadors/).

## Eligibility and Selection

To be eligible, nominees must:

* Be at least 18 years old
* Follow the [PyTorch Foundation Code of Conduct](https://pytorch.org/code-of-conduct/) and [Linux Foundation Antitrust Policy](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/legal/antitrust-policy)
* Demonstrate technical knowledge of PyTorch through open-source contributions, published content, or community leadership.
* Commit to participating for a two-year term.

Ambassador nominations will be evaluated on the following criteria:

* Community impact and engagement
* Technical expertise and thought leadership
* Consistent activity within PyTorch Foundation projects and communities
* Commitment to openness, inclusion, and collaboration

## A Global Community

The PyTorch Foundation is seeking Ambassadors from a broad range of regions and communities to help build a globally representative program. Nominees will be asked to share their location to help identify opportunities for regional engagement and support across PyTorch Foundation projects and communities.

The PyTorch Ambassador Program is an exciting chapter in our community's growth. We invite you to [join us](https://pytorch.org/programs/ambassadors/application-process/) in building an even more connected, inclusive, and global ecosystem.
