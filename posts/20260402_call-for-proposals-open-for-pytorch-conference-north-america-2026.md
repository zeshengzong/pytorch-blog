# Call for Proposals Open for PyTorch Conference North America 2026

**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Author:** PyTorch Foundation

## Overview

The PyTorch Foundation is officially opening the Call for Proposals (CFP) for PyTorch Conference North America 2026, held **October 20–21 in San Jose, California**. This premier event gathers AI pioneers, researchers, and developers to explore the future of open source AI and PyTorch Foundation projects including PyTorch, vLLM, DeepSpeed, and Ray.

## Technical Tracks and Submission Categories

Proposals are being accepted for **breakout sessions and posters** emphasizing implementation details, architectural insights, and developer tooling across these tracks:

- **Core PyTorch:** Evolution of the framework and internal architectural changes
- **Kernel Engineering:** Compilers, optimization strategies, and domain-specific languages (DSLs)
- **Inference and Training:** Techniques, libraries, and optimizations for large-scale model execution
- **Applications:** Technical case studies showcasing PyTorch ecosystem deployments in production environments
- **Responsible AI:** Engineering approaches to ethics, governance, security, and privacy sandboxing
