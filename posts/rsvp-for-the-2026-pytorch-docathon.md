# RSVP for the 2026 PyTorch Docathon

**By:** Team PyTorch  
**Date:** April 3, 2026

## Overview

The 2026 PyTorch Docathon will take place **May 5-19**. This is a hackathon-style event aimed at enhancing PyTorch documentation with community support. Documentation is a critical component of any technology, and refining it simplifies the onboarding process for new users, helps them effectively utilize PyTorch's features, and speeds up the transition from research to production in machine learning.

## Why Participate

**Low Barrier to Entry**

Unlike many open-source projects that require deep knowledge of the codebase and previous contributions, the Docathon is tailored for newcomers. While participants should be familiar with Python and have basic knowledge of PyTorch and machine learning, there are tasks related to website issues that don't require that level of expertise.

**Tangible Results**

A major advantage of the Docathon is witnessing the immediate impact of your contributions. Enhancing documentation significantly boosts a project's usability and accessibility, and you'll observe these improvements directly. Seeing tangible outcomes is a strong motivator to continue contributing.

**Learning Opportunities**

Even if you're not a PyTorch expert, the Docathon offers valuable learning experiences. You'll delve into PyTorch modules, test tutorials on your machine, and explore them in the CI environment.

## Who Should Participate

Whether you're a seasoned documentation expert or just starting out, everyone is invited to join the PyTorch Docathon to contribute and develop skills and knowledge to improve documentation for everyone. Issues will be labeled by skill level, and the PyTorch Discord will be available for collaboration and help.

## Event Schedule

- **May 5:** Kick-off at 10 AM PT
- **May 6 – May 15:** Submissions and Feedback
- **May 16 – May 18:** Final Reviews
- **May 20:** Winner Announcements
