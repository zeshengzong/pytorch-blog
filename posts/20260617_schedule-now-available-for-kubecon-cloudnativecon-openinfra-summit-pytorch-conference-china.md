# Schedule Now Available for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon + OpenInfra Summit + PyTorch Conference China

**By PyTorch Foundation**
**June 17, 2026**

AI is transforming how we build, deploy, and operate technology. Open source is making it possible.

[KubeCon + CloudNativeCon + OpenInfra Summit + PyTorch Conference China](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/) will take place September 7-9 in [Shanghai](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/attend/venue-travel/).

[The schedule is now available](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/), featuring engineers, maintainers, researchers, and technology leaders advancing cloud native infrastructure, open infrastructure, and AI.

## Session Highlights

- [Scaling Digital Employees at China Merchants Bank in Production: A Controlled AI Agents Approach](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1225364) — Jiahang Xu, China Merchants Bank
- [How Intsig Serves Billions of Document Scans: GPU Virtualization at Scale with HAMi](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1221808) — Xiao Zhang, dynamia.ai and Walter Duan, instig information co. ltd.
- [Spec-Driven Evolution: How AI Agents are Rewriting the OpenStack/K8s Playbook](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1224932) — Bertran Souveill, DOCOMO Euro-Labs and Yuya Kuno, NTT DOCOMO
- [From Cloud to AI: How Kata Containers 4.0 Reinvents the Sandbox for the Agent Era](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1220661) — Fupan Li, Ant Group
- [The State of the PyTorch Ecosystem in 2026: Global Trends and China's Rising Role](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1225138) — Wei Wang, East China Normal University
- [Training Through Failures: How Meta Keeps 100k-GPU Jobs Alive with Open-Source Fault Tolerance](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/program/schedule/?id=1223450) — Tristan Rice and Amir Afzali, Meta

## Co-Located Events

### OSPOlogy + OSPO Summit China

[OSPOlogy + OSPO Summit China](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/co-located-events/ospology-ospo-summit/) will take place on September 7. The half-day event covers the evolution of Open Source Program Management (OSPO) functions and corporate open source governance in the era of Agentic AI.

[Registration details](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/co-located-events/ospology-ospo-summit/#registration-details) and the [call for proposals](https://www.lfopensource.cn/kubecon-cloudnativecon-openinfra-summit-pytorch-conference-china/co-located-events/ospology-ospo-summit/#call-for-proposals) are available online.

### AGNTCon + MCPCon China

[AGNTCon + MCPCon China](https://www.lfopensource.cn/mcp-dev-summit-shanghai/) will take place September 6-7 ahead of the main event. The event focuses on building reliable, scalable, and secure agent systems in practice.

[Registration information is available here](https://www.lfopensource.cn/mcp-dev-summit-shanghai/register/).
